Forging India’s Quantum Legacy

Our Standing

QPIN stands as India’s premier quantum entity, backed by the Ministry of Science and Technology, with a proven track record of delivering cutting-edge systems on time and budget. Our 100-qubit processor, operational by December 2026, marks the beginning—a springboard to rival IBM’s 1,121-qubit Condor (2027) and Google’s Willow (2024) in efficiency and impact. With coherence times exceeding 120 µs, fidelities above 99.9%, and a scalable architecture, we’re poised to leapfrog global benchmarks, training India’s quantum pioneers to dominate the field by 2045.

Projected Roadmap: 2025-2045

From 100 Qubits to Global Leadership

2025-2026: Foundation & Kickoff

  • Activity: Establish R&D labs, hire 50 quantum experts (₹50 crore).
  • Milestone: Launch "Quantum India Initiative" (QII)—train 500 students (₹10 crore).
  • Focus: Benchmark 100-qubit RCS vs. IBM Eagle, Google Willow (10²⁵ years classical).
  • Outcome: India’s first quantum talent pipeline begins.

2027-2028: Early Scaling

  • Activity: Upgrade to 200 qubits (₹300 crore), fault-tolerant prototypes.
  • Milestone: Solve 50-bit ECDLP with Shor’s (<1 hour, 10¹⁵ years classical).
  • Training: Expand QII to 2,000 trainees with IITs/IISc (₹25 crore).
  • Outcome: Joins 200-qubit club, matches IBM Osprey scale.

2029-2030: Mid-Scale Breakthrough

  • Activity: Deploy 300 qubits (₹405 crore), error rate <0.1%.
  • Milestone: Optimize Indian Railways logistics (10x faster, ₹500 crore savings).
  • Training: Train 5,000 via "Quantum Corps" (₹50 crore).
  • Outcome: Rivals Google’s 2030 goals with practical applications.

2031-2033: Fault-Tolerance Threshold

  • Activity: Scale to 500 qubits (₹800 crore), 100 logical qubits.
  • Milestone: Crack 128-bit AES (<1 day), post-quantum urgency.
  • Training: Quantum Corps to 10,000, 50 PhDs/year (₹100 crore).
  • Outcome: Matches IBM’s 2033 fault-tolerance, leads in security.

2034-2036: Quantum Advantage Era

  • Activity: Reach 1000 qubits (₹1500 crore), 250 logical qubits.
  • Milestone: Simulate nitrogen fixation catalysts (20% cost reduction).
  • Training: National Quantum Academy (NQA), 25,000 trained (₹200 crore).
  • Outcome: Leapfrogs Google’s 2035 vision, solves national issues.

2037-2040: Quantum Supremacy

  • Activity: Deploy 10,000 qubits (₹5000 crore), 1000+ logical qubits.
  • Milestone: Break secp256k1 (<1 week), global crypto shift.
  • Training: NQA graduates 50,000, 500 startups (₹300 crore).
  • Outcome: Equals IBM’s 2040 scale, leads in cryptography.

2041-2045: Global Quantum Leadership

  • Activity: Scale to 100,000 qubits (₹15,000 crore), error ~10⁻⁹.
  • Milestone: Optimize energy grid (15% loss cut, ₹1 lakh crore savings).
  • Training: 100,000 workforce, host World Quantum Summit (₹500 crore).
  • Outcome: Surpasses IBM/Google, quantum R&D hub.
