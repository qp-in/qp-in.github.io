Success Stories

2025: Zurich Kit Integration

Partnered with Zurich Instruments to integrate the Quantum Kit into our 100-qubit system, achieving a two-qubit gate error rate of 0.1%—a milestone that sets the stage for RCS benchmarking against IBM’s Condor by 2026.

2026: IIT Quantum Corps Launch

Collaborated with IIT Bombay to train 2,000 students in quantum programming, launching the Quantum Corps—a talent pool driving India’s 200-qubit upgrade by 2028.

2027: AWS Cloud Deployment

With AWS, deployed a secure cloud platform for our 100-qubit processor, enabling researchers nationwide to run Shor’s algorithm in <10 minutes, showcased at the QPIN Quantum Summit.
