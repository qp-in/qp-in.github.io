Featured Project: 100-Qubit Quantum Processor

Budget: ₹369 Crore | Completion: December 1, 2026

QPIN is spearheading a groundbreaking ₹369 crore initiative to deliver a state-of-the-art 100-qubit quantum processor by December 2026. Launched on June 2, 2025, this 18-month project procures a high-performance chip, installs it in a world-class lab, and operates it with a skilled team.

Why It Matters: Outpacing global benchmarks at lower cost, it runs advanced algorithms—factoring numbers in minutes, simulating molecules in hours—paving the way for breakthroughs.

What’s Next: Scaling to 1000+ qubits by 2035, cementing India’s quantum leadership.
